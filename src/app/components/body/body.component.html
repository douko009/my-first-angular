PICKING YOUR
CHAMPION AND ROLES

In League Of Legends, there are a lot of characters or champions as they call it. Each champtions have their own design, abilities and roles. Picking a champion can be hard since it's all about your play style. There are five roles in the game; Top laner, Mid Laner, Attack Damage Carry (ADC), Jungler and Support. Each roles have different impact in the game that you can use in order to win a match! You can learn more about the Champions and their Roles HERE.

OBJECTIVES

It is very easy to tell which team has an upper hand as the game continues because of the number of objectives they took during the match. Getting objectives like Dragons, Rift Herald and Barron can give you a very big potential of winning the game, so do you best to capture more objectives for your team!

WIN A MATCH

To WIN and END the match in League of Legends, you have to push your opponents turret, destroy their enhibitors and finish off the their NEXUS. Of course this isn't that easy to do if you are facing a very competitive oppotent. But just like the those other online games, TEAM WORK is the key to achieve an overwhelming VICTORY!
